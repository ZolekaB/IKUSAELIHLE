Who We Are

Ikusaselihle Consultants (Pty) Ltd (ICE) is a dynamic black female-owned Civil and Structural Consulting Firm, nounded in 2018 by Ms. Londwayinkosi Nokubonga Mhlongo, we offer a comprehensive suite of services,including pavement design, road rehabilitation, housing projects, and structural engineering solutions. Our expertise spans periodic and routine maintenance, civil and structural design, construction supervision, and project management.

At ICE, we bridge gaps, not just in infrastructure but in the consulting sector, uplifting communities through cost-effective, ethical services. Committed to public safety, we bring excellence and integrity to the core of our service provision. With a track record of successful projects, we collaborate with esteemed clients like SANRAL, KwaZulu Natal Department of Transport, EThekwini Municipality, and the Department of Public Works.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission

At Ikusaselihle Consultants (Pty) Ltd (ICE), we are the architects of transformation, the builders of resilience, and the stewards of progress in the world of civil and structural engineering. Our journey began in 2018, guided by a steadfast commitment to excellence, integrity, and community upliftment. We believe in bridges, not just as structures that span rivers and roads, but as symbols of connection, unity, and opportunity. Our black female-owned firm stands as a beacon, bridging the gaps in the consulting sector, empowering communities, and nurturing the talents of our people.With precision and care, we craft pavement designs that pave the way for safe journeys, rehabilitate roads to breathe new life into infrastructure, and design housing projects that offer homes and hope. Our periodic and routine maintenance ensures longevity, and our civil and structural designs, from bridges to culverts, stand as testaments to innovation.

To provide exceptional civil and structural consulting services in the country, upholding excellence and integrity as the core values of our service provision. We envision a future where our innovative engineering solutions not only meet the highest industry standards but also lead in sustainable design practices, making a significant contribution to environmental preservation. Our commitment extends to fostering a diverse and inclusive workforce that thrives on creativity and collaboration, ultimately shaping a world where our legacy is measured not just by structures but by the positive impact we leave on society.

Our mission is to bridge a gap in the built environment consulting sector, leveraging the expertise of black-owned professionals and consultants. We are dedicated to providing professional and ethical services, guided not only by relevant laws and principles but also by our unwavering commitment to community development. Through our work, we aim to create opportunities, promote inclusivity, and ensure sustainable, transformative impacts that resonate far beyond the realms of consulting
